# 🎮 Game Hub

A collection of fun, interactive web-based games built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript!

## 📁 Games Included

### 🐦 Flappy Bird
Navigate through pipes without hitting them. Choose your difficulty and try to reach your target score!

**Features:**
- ✨ Three difficulty levels:
  - Easy: Target 5 points
  - Normal: Target 10 points
  - Hard: Target 20 points
- 🎨 Beautiful gradient background with animated clouds
- 🎯 Real-time score tracking
- 🏆 Win screen when you reach your target
- ⚡ Smooth physics and animations

**Controls:**
- **SPACE** - Jump/Start/Restart/Play Again
- **← HOME** - Return to Game Hub

**How to Play:**
1. Open `FlappyBird.html` in your browser
2. Select your difficulty level
3. Press SPACE to start
4. Press SPACE to jump and avoid the pipes
5. Reach your target score to win!

---

### 🎾 PONG
Classic arcade game for 2 players! Compete against a friend with customizable winning scores.

**Features:**
- 👥 Two-player local multiplayer
- ⚙️ Customizable "First to" score (1-100)
- 🌟 Neon cyberpunk aesthetic
- ✨ Particle effects on ball hits
- 🎯 Real-time score display
- ⏸️ Pause functionality

**Controls:**
- **Player 1 (Cyan Paddle):**
  - **W** - Move up
  - **S** - Move down

- **Player 2 (Magenta Paddle):**
  - **↑ (Up Arrow)** - Move up
  - **↓ (Down Arrow)** - Move down

- **Game Controls:**
  - **SPACE** - Start/Play Again/Resume
  - **P** - Pause/Resume
  - **← HOME** - Return to Game Hub

**How to Play:**
1. Open `Pong.html` in your browser
2. Enter your desired "First to" score (default: 5)
3. Press SPACE to start
4. Control your paddle to hit the ball
5. First player to reach the target score wins!

## 🎮 Game Tips

### Flappy Bird Tips
- Time your jumps to navigate smoothly through pipes
- The higher difficulty levels have tighter gaps
- Watch the clouds - they're just decoration!
- Practice makes perfect!

### PONG Tips
- Predict where the ball will go
- Hit the ball near the edge of your paddle for sharper angles
- Work together with your friend for longer rallies
- The ball speed increases with each hit
- Customize the winning score for shorter or longer games

## 🎓 How to Run

**Option 1: Direct File**
- Simply open any(preferabbly `index.html` for homepage) `.html` file directly in your browser

**Option 2: Local Server** (Recommended for best experience)
```bash
# Python 3
python -m http.server 8000

# Python 2
python -m SimpleHTTPServer 8000

# Node.js (with http-server)
npx http-server
```

Then navigate to `http://localhost:8000` and click on `Games/` folder
